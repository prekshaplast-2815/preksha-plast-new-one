About Preksha Plast

Global Sourcing • Import • Export • Merchant Trading

Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd. is a professionally managed India-based Global Sourcing, Import, Export and Merchant Trading Company engaged in the sourcing, import, export, supply and global distribution of Plastic Raw Materials, Agro Commodities and Industrial Chemicals. We are committed to delivering premium-quality products through reliable procurement, transparent business practices and an efficient global supply chain.

Our operations are strategically organized into three specialized business divisions – Plastic Division, Agro Division and Chemical Division. Each division is supported by experienced sourcing professionals, trusted suppliers and industry expertise, enabling us to serve manufacturers, processors, traders and international buyers with dependable sourcing solutions tailored to their specific requirements.

Our Plastic Division specializes in Plastic Raw Materials, Engineering Plastics, Prime, Near Prime, Off-Grade, Reprocessed Materials, Regrind, Plastic Rolls, Films, Lumps and allied industrial materials. Our Agro Division focuses on premium Agro Commodities, while our Chemical Division supplies Industrial Chemicals and allied raw materials for diverse industrial applications.

Backed by a strong global supplier network, stringent quality inspection procedures, efficient warehousing, import-export expertise and dependable logistics management, we ensure consistent quality, competitive pricing, timely deliveries and uninterrupted supply across domestic and international markets.

At Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd., our mission is to build long-term business partnerships by delivering reliable sourcing solutions, responsive customer support and sustainable value for our clients. Guided by integrity, transparency and customer satisfaction, we strive to become a trusted global partner in sourcing, import, export and international merchant trade.

Careers

At Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd., we believe that people are the foundation of long-term business growth. We are always interested in connecting with dedicated, skilled and growth-oriented professionals who can contribute to our sourcing, logistics and trading operations.

We offer opportunities in procurement, quality control, export coordination, logistics management, sales support and business development across Plastic, Rubber, Chemical and Food product divisions.

Our work environment encourages responsibility, professionalism, teamwork and continuous learning. We value integrity, customer commitment and practical problem-solving in day-to-day operations.

If you are interested in building a career with Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd., please share your profile and experience details with us. We welcome talented individuals who wish to grow with a dynamic and expanding business platform.

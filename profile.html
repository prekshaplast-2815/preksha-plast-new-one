Company Profile

Established in 2017, Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd. is a professionally managed Global Sourcing, Import, Export, and Inspection Company headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. With years of industry experience, the company has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable sourcing solutions and maintaining the highest standards of quality, transparency, and professionalism.

We specialize in the sourcing and global supply of Plastic Raw Materials, Agro Commodities, and Industrial Chemicals, serving customers across domestic and international markets. Our extensive supplier network, strategic procurement capabilities, and strict quality inspection processes enable us to consistently deliver products that meet international standards and customer expectations.

Our business philosophy is centered on integrity, long-term partnerships, and customer satisfaction. We work closely with manufacturers, suppliers, and buyers to provide competitive pricing, reliable procurement, and efficient international trade solutions while ensuring complete transparency throughout the sourcing process.

At Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd., we continuously strive to strengthen our global presence by building trusted business relationships and delivering sustainable value to customers worldwide through professionalism, innovation, and excellence in international trade.

Plastic Division

Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd. specializes in the sourcing, supply and global distribution of Plastic Raw Materials, Engineering Plastics and Recyclable Plastic Materials for domestic and international markets.

Our Plastic Division offers a comprehensive range of materials including HDPE, LDPE, LLDPE, PP, PET, PVC, ABS, PC, PA (Nylon), POM, PMMA, Engineering Plastics, Prime, Near Prime, Off-Grade, Floor Sweep, Reprocessed Materials, Regrind, Plastic Rolls, Plastic Films, Lumps and Purgings to meet diverse industrial requirements.

With an extensive supplier network, stringent quality inspection procedures, efficient warehousing and reliable logistics management, we ensure consistent quality, competitive pricing, timely deliveries and uninterrupted supply across every order.

We serve industries including Packaging, Automotive, Electrical & Electronics, Infrastructure, Consumer Goods and Manufacturing, delivering reliable sourcing solutions tailored to customer requirements. Our objective is to build long-term business partnerships through quality materials, transparent business practices and dependable global sourcing solutions.

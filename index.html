Welcome to Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2017, Preksha Plast Pvt. Ltd. is a professionally managed India-based Global Sourcing, Import, Export, and Inspection Company specializing in Plastic Raw Materials, Agro Commodities, and Industrial Chemicals.

Backed by a strong global supplier network, extensive market expertise, and an unwavering commitment to quality, we deliver reliable sourcing, competitive procurement, quality inspection, and international trade solutions to customers across domestic and international markets.

Driven by integrity, transparency, and customer satisfaction, we are committed to building long-term business partnerships by delivering dependable sourcing and international trade solutions across our three core divisions — Plastic, Agro, and Industrial Chemicals.
